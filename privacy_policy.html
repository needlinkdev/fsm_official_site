Section 1

Information We Collect

When you register or use Fresher Study Master, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Email Address – for account creation, login, and communication.
  • Username – for identification within the App.
  • Password – securely stored for account authentication.
  • Phone Number – may be used for account recovery and communication.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as government IDs, financial details, or health information.

Section 2

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide access to lecture materials and past questions.
  • Manage and secure user accounts.
  • Communicate updates, support messages, or important notices.
  • Improve the App’s functionality and user experience.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Section 3

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your password is encrypted and not visible to us.

Section 4

Data Sharing

We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate the App (e.g., Firebase for authentication and data storage).
Section 5

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Section 6

Children’s Privacy

Fresher Study Master is intended for university students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Section 7

Your Privacy Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Contact us regarding any privacy concerns.
Section 8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the App and on our Play Store listing. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the revised policy.
